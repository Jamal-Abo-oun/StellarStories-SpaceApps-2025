🌞 Space Fun Facts

🌌 What’s in Space?

Space has stars, planets, moons and even floating rocks!

⚡ Solar Flares

The Sun sometimes shoots out big bursts of energy, These light up space.

🌬️ Solar Wind

The Sun blows tiny particles called solar wind. It flows through space like a gentle invisible breeze.

📡 Effects on Earth

Solar flares and wind can shake up satellites, radios, and even electricity.

🌈 Magical Auroras

A colorful lights called auroras — like magic dancing above us!
