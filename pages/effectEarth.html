📡 How Solar Flares and Solar Wind Affect Earth(Aurora)

🌍 What Happens on Earth?

Solar flares and the solar wind send streams of energy and charged particles toward Earth. These can create stunning auroras near the poles, disrupt satellites, affect GPS and radio communications, and sometimes even cause power outages. Scientists watch the Sun carefully to protect our technology and keep everyone safe.
